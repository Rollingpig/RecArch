The Arario Museum in Space is a modern art museum that was established by the chairman of the Arario Corporation, Chang-il Kim. The building that houses the museum was designed by the famed Korean architect Kim Swoo-geun. Until he passed away in 1986, the building served as Kim's office and home. Today, the Arario Museum in Space calls the building home and the spaces are full of incredible modern art. This historic building is one of the most unique cultural spaces in Seoul.
